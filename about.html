About Athkar Bot

Learn more about Athkar Bot, its purpose, features, and how it can benefit your Discord community.

What is Athkar Bot?

Athkar Bot is a Discord bot designed to provide Islamic remembrances (Athkar) to Discord communities. It helps Muslims maintain their spiritual practice by delivering daily morning and evening Athkar automatically, as well as providing on-demand reminders through simple commands.

The word "Athkar" (أذكار) is the plural form of "Thikr" (ذكر) which means "remembrance" in Arabic. In Islamic tradition, these are specific phrases and prayers that Muslims recite to remember and glorify Allah (God).

How Does It Work?

Scheduled Athkar

Athkar Bot automatically sends morning Athkar at 6:00 AM and evening Athkar at 6:00 PM to the designated channel in your server. These times are set to help Muslims maintain the tradition of morning and evening remembrances.

Command-Based Athkar

Users can request Athkar at any time using the following commands:

!athkar - Get a random Athkar

!athkar morning - Get a morning Athkar

!athkar evening - Get an evening Athkar

Benefits for Your Community

  • Regular spiritual reminders without manual effort
  • Encourages consistent Islamic practice
  • Creates a positive atmosphere in your Discord server
  • Helps members remember Allah throughout their day
  • Educational content for those learning about Islamic practices
  • Simple to set up and use with no complicated configuration

Technical Details

Athkar Bot is built using Python with the Discord.py library. It runs on reliable cloud infrastructure to ensure consistent uptime and performance. The bot requires minimal permissions to function, only needing the ability to send messages in your server's channels.

The bot collects only necessary data to function, such as server and channel IDs. For more information about data collection and usage, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
